Instructions

The goal of ELUS is to correctly choose which figure comes next in line.

The first 3 figures are laid out for you.

Find the next 5 figures without making more than 3 errors.

A hidden rule determines which figure comes next...

   

...based on an attribute of the last figure:

Size: Small or Big

Color: Blue or Yellow

Shape: Circle or Lozenge

There are 3 choices in each turn, but only one is correct, the others do not follow the rule.

Whether your choice is right or wrong, the rule will apply to your selection and the next choices will be based on it.

In a Classic game there are 3 levels, each with a more difficult rule than the previous one. Hover over LEVEL to get some hints about the rule.

In a Random game there is 1 level, and the rule is randomly generated.

Good luck!
